About Me

Educator & Technologist

I am an Associate Professor at Kabul Education University's Faculty of Computer Science with over 10 years of experience in both academia and software development. My passion lies in bridging theoretical knowledge with practical application.

As a Full Stack Developer specializing, I've developed numerous web applications and systems that solve real-world problems. I bring this industry experience into my classroom to provide students with relevant, up-to-date knowledge.

My research interests include software engineering best practices, modern web technologies, and artificial intelligence with a strong focus on data science. I am particularly passionate about exploring how these areas can be applied to solve real-world problems and drive innovation. In addition, I am committed to advancing both the field of computer science and the quality of education in Afghanistan by fostering research, promoting digital literacy, and empowering the next generation of technology leaders..
